What we do

We are about thinking and doing, strategy and implementation. We combine specialist skills in operations, finance, people management, strategy and technology with extensive industry experience to make a difference to the operational performance of our public and private sector clients.

What will you be doing?

• You will develop an in-depth understanding of clients and their industries

• You may have the opportunity to travel to client sites in other parts of the country

• You will test efficiency of business processes and identify areas for improvement

• You will have the opportunity to present work (‘deliverables’) and recommendations back to clients with your project team

• You will be applying Deloitte methodologies to help clients streamline, improve, innovate and implement

• You will apply data analysis and modelling to help clients make important decisions for their organistation

• You will present your work and findings back to partners

• You will work with your project team to recommend best practice, and build all aspects of a business or process

• You may have the chance to engage directly with a client’s customers or service users, to understand how the client can better meet their needs

• You will help to produce professional, visually engaging deliverables for clients using design tools and skills
